Features

Everything you need on the ground and in the air

📡

D-ATIS

Live Digital ATIS for 89 D-ATIS airports across the US and Canada. Departure, arrival, and combined feeds — always current.

🌦

METAR & TAF

Full raw METAR and TAF with color-coded formatting and local time conversion for any airport in the world.

👁

RVR

Live Runway Visual Range for all instrumented runways, with touchdown, midpoint, and rollout readings and 60-minute trend charts.

⏱

GDP & Ground Stops

Real-time FAA Ground Delay Programs and Ground Stops with delays, impacting conditions, and departure facility scope.

🔔

Push Notifications

Subscribe to ATIS change alerts for any airport. Get notified the moment the information code changes, with an AI-generated summary of what changed.

📍

Nearby Airports

Automatically surfaces D-ATIS airports within your chosen radius using GPS — no searching required.

📜

History

Browse ATIS and METAR history by date, and review the full lifecycle of any active GDP or Ground Stop program.

🛫

Runway Config

Live arrival and departure runway configurations from FAA APTC data, shown alongside each ATIS card.
